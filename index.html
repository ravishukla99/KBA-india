About Us

Established as a Proprietor Firm in the year 2020 at Surat (Gujarat, India), we "Kashtabhanjan Automation" known as "KBA" is a leading solution provider of a wide range of products including Servo Motor, Linear Guideway, Vacuum, Pneumatic, programmable logic controller etc. In addition, we also offer best-in-class robotics, repairing services, automation system development services and customized machine solutions. Kashtabhanjan Automation is committed to generating growth to meet customer demand. Such growth is only possible by collaborating with various business units within an organization to deliver on globally challenging customer demands. KBA delivers the right products, services and support at ever-decreasing costs.

Today, we meet the total lifecycle needs of the entire company, from New Panels to Robots, Service and Repair adding value at every touch point to help customers realize the most value from their investment. To best address the business needs of our Industrial Customers, we offer a variety of products and services.
